Brand Identity and Corporate Design

An effective brand identity design communicates the vision, mission, and culture that is behind the company and its product to the World. It engages all senses and it can be seen, felt, heard, savored, and smelled. The brand identity project takes all elements of the puzzle and brings them together into a cohesive structure.
With its talented and experimented graphic designers, Alive Advertise guides you and helps you:

  • Knowing your market and its current trends
  • Express your visual identity
  • Being surrounded by graphic designers

We can help you design your Logo, Flyer, Business cards, Brochures, Flyers, Letterheads, Envelopes, Billboards, Stands, and many more.

At Alive Advertise, we specialize in the design of the visual and web interactive parts of the brand identity.

Our usual brand identity design project involves creating

An appealing and timeless logo

Effective collateral materials

Action-driving brochures and flyers

Promotional materials

Packaging that sells

Professional signage

Engaging websites
