WEBSITES DEVELOPMENT

  • DO YOU CREATE CUSTOMIZED WEBSITE DESIGNS?

    Yes, our team are highly qualified to create customized creative website designs from scratch. We specialize in understanding our clients and businesses need and we create specific designs accordingly. In addition, we provide already created template designs for faster delivery, basic website needs and lower cost. By working with Webneoo, you will have different options that will match your need and budget.

  • WHAT IS YOUR WORKING METHODOLOGY?

    Alive Advertise deliver high-end projects by going through, what we call, the path to success.
    The road of each project starts from the "Analysis and Design" phase by breaking down the project into simpler smaller units and drawing a conceptual database design.
    Moving into the fun phase of "Development", where the code is written and tested before integrating it in the system.
    After that comes the "Data Integration" and "Beta Testing" phases, where the real data is uploaded and the project is tested by deploying it on a testing server. Finally, the "Deployment" phase where the world gets to see the marvelous artwork.

  • HOW MUCH TIME DO YOU NEED TO DELIVER THE PROJECT?

    Depends on each project. Some projects takes our team few days. While some takes several months of dedicated hard work.

  • DO YOU PROVIDE TECHNICAL SUPPORT?

    Of course, Alive Advertise provides technical support depending on the type of project and the package chosen.

  • IS THERE ANY HIDDEN COST THAT WE WILL DISCOVER DURING THE PROJECT?

    Never. The relationship between Alive Advertise and our clients is extremely transparent and smooth.

  • HOW MANY PEOPLE WILL WORK ON MY PROJECT?

    Depends on the project. However, there will definitely be at least a project manager and a technical expert working on every project no matter how tiny the project is.

  • WHAT IS THE PRICING METHODOLOGY THAT YOU USE TO ESTIMATE A PROJECT VALUE?

    Our team prices projects according to the features required.
