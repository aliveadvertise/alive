UI/UX Design

10 seconds is the time needed for a user to leave or stay on a website. This is the time we have to seduce our user and convince him to stay on our website.

  • Increased customer loyalty
  • Brand consistency
  • Higher converions rates

UI is responsible for the graphic design that ends with an easy and pleasant to the eye aesthetic, as well as intuitive functionalities. Both aspects work closely together to enhance the user's experiences and are conducive to conversions.
At Alive Advertise, we built our own UX methodologies based on Design Thinking. Building a good UX requires the co-operation of UX Designers, UI Designers, Integrators, Developers and Client!

How to build a good UX?

Alive Advertise uses a UX methodology that follows the below steps:

Understand the user needs
and expectations

Conceive the first functionalities of your product based on the results of the last step

Create a structure by selecting the elements that should be present to satisfy your users

Create
wireframes

Establish focus group to get feedback from your users and update your product base on it

Re-iterate the focus group until getting a prototype with your user's expectations

Design the user interface
